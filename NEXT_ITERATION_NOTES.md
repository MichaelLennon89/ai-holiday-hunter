# AI Holiday Hunter v24

## What changed
- Whole deal cards are clickable and open the provider page.
- Background jobs now show a real progress bar, elapsed time, and estimated time remaining.
- Map legend is in plain English and is clickable to filter destinations by price band.
- Result cards now behave more like travel-site listings.

## Run
Double-click `START_AI_Holiday_Hunter.bat` on Windows.

## Notes
This iteration focuses on interaction quality and live-run transparency rather than changing the core search engine.
